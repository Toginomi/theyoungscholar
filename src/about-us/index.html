About The Young Scholar

Introduction

The Young Scholar is a premier NCE Review Program dedicated to empowering the next generation of Filipino scientists, innovators, and leaders. Founded and led by alumni of the Philippine Science High School (Pisay), our platform serves as a bridge between aspiring students and the world-class education offered by the PSHS System.

We believe that every student, regardless of their background, possesses a unique spark of genius and the potential to achieve their Pisay dreams. To turn that potential into reality, we provide an integrated learning experience that goes beyond static handouts. Our program offers high-quality, alumni-vetted curriculum, interactive AI-powered diagnostics, and a supportive community of mentors who have successfully walked the path themselves.

Our mission is to democratize elite-level education. We are committed to fostering academic excellence, critical thinking, and the confidence required to ace the National Competitive Examination (NCE). At The Young Scholar, we aren't just teaching subjects; we are nurturing the future pioneers of Philippine science and technology.
