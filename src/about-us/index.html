About The Young Scholar

Introduction

The Young Scholar Hub is dedicated to empowering the next generation of Filipino scientists. We provide accessible, high-quality review materials created by Pisay Alumni to help students ace the NCE.


We believe that every student has the potential to achieve their Pisay dreams. We are committed to fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills that serve students beyond just the exams.
