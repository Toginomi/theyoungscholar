What we do

The Young Scholar Hub provides comprehensive preparation for the Philippine Science High School National Competitive Exam (NCE). Our programs cover all key areas of the NCE, including in-depth review of math, science, verbal aptitude, and logical reasoning, along with proven test-taking strategies, practice exams, and personalized feedback from experienced instructors. We aim to unlock each student's full potential and guide them on the path to Pisay success.
